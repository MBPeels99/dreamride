Welcome to the extraordinary world of Dream Ride

The safe social network for creative children which develops kids imagination and cognitive developing

You have entered a magical place where creativity knows no bounds and imagination takes flight! Prepare to embark on a thrilling journey filled with magic, friendship, and endless possibilities.

Dream Ride is not just an ordinary social network; it's a vibrant community that revolves around a captivating pop-up book. This interactive book is not merely meant to be read, but to be experienced and built together, fostering a sense of wonder and discovery. With its elements to cut out, fold, and stick, children become active participants in the creation of the book itself. As they engage in this process, their concentration, fine motor skills, and imagination soar to new heights.

Get ready to join Bobby and his fantastical hat as they explore vibrant new worlds and forge unforgettable connections.

In the first edition of the Dream Ride pop-up book, Bobby's adventures unfold before your eyes. When Bobby falls asleep, his hat becomes a gateway to an enchanting dream world. Every night, a different story unfolds, and Bobby finds himself whisked away to extraordinary places across the universe. Along the way, he encounters whimsical characters, engages in hilarious situations, and forms deep bonds with newfound friends. Bobby's dreams become a second life, brimming with magic, imagination, and cherished memories.

In the enchanting realm of Dream Ride, a Bulgarian author's extraordinary journey unfolds. Born from the depths of author's creative spirit, this captivating book emerged as a university project, showcasing remarkable talent and passion. The pictures on this website proudly represent the original Bulgarian version.

Author, illustrator, paper-engineer, pre-press preparation and book's prototype creator - Martina Dumbarova.

 
 

From book to social network
A big step forward

Now, let us introduce you to the heart of Dream Ride — the online platform that connects creative children from all corners of the globe. Here, young and talented story writers, illustrators, and paper engineers gather to bring Bobby's adventures to life. Imagine the thrill of collaborating with fellow young visionaries, exchanging ideas, and shaping the destiny of the Dream Ride universe.

 
 

Creative kids - Talents community

 

Story writers

Is your child an aspiring storyteller with a brilliant plot twist up his sleeve?

Dream Ride welcomes young writers to submit their creations. Let your talented child share his stories. And enjoy how his imagination takes centre stage for the next exciting book.

The story writer's community is full of enthusiastic young minds. They provide feedback and support, helping your child refine and shape his narrative.

 

Illustrators

Perhaps your child has an eye for artistry and a knack for illustrating captivating characters.

Dream Ride invites little illustrators to submit their drawings, breathing life into Bobby, his hat, and the colourful inhabitants of their dream world.

Let your kid's artistic talents shine as he envisions breathtaking landscapes, vibrant cityscapes, and quirky creatures that will captivate readers of all ages.

 

Paper engineers

May exist a pop-up book without paper engineers?
Of course not.

Experienced paper engineers are here to inspire and educate your child. Through engaging videos, they'll introduce little engineers' mind to captivating techniques and unveil the secrets behind the creation of mesmerizing pop-up wonders.

As a member of the Dream Ride community, your kid can embark on paper tasks, replicating what he's learned or unleashing his creativity to design his own paper masterpieces.

 

Translators

Dream Ride also believes in the power of language to transcend borders.

Translators of all ages are encouraged to participate, providing proposals for translating captivating stories into different languages. By embracing diversity and accessibility, Dream Ride aims to bring the magic of this creative children's social network to a wider audience, uniting young minds from various cultures and nations.
