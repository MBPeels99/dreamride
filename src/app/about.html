About the book

In the book market where innovation often takes a back seat, Dream Ride emerges as a trailblazer, offering an immersive experience unlike any other. Unlike traditional pop-up books that arrive complete and ready to explore, our unique approach involves children in the exciting journey of assembling the book themselves.

Through cutting, folding, and sticking, young minds become active participants in the creation of their very own Dream Ride adventure. This interactive process not only teaches them about the inner workings of pop-up books but also hones their fine motor skills, concentration, and attention to detail.

 
 

Get ready to join Bobby and his fantastical hat as they explore vibrant new worlds and forge unforgettable connections.

In the first edition of the Dream Ride pop-up book, Bobby's adventures unfold before your eyes. When Bobby falls asleep, his hat becomes a gateway to an enchanting dream world. Every night, a different story unfolds, and Bobby finds himself whisked away to extraordinary places across the universe. Along the way, he encounters whimsical characters, engages in hilarious situations, and forms deep bonds with newfound friends. Bobby's dreams become a second life, brimming with magic, imagination, and cherished memories.

Activities

    Following instructions
    Cutting out elements
    Folding elements
    Taping element on their places

Helps improving

    Fine motoric skills
    Attention focus
    Precision and eye on details

Recommended age

  Age 5 - 8
        Age 8+
 
 
 

Book - gateway to social network

Dream Ride is more than just a book series - it is a gateway to a vibrant social network that sparks collaboration and unleashes the power of collective imagination. Once parents sign up and register their children, young dreamers gain access to a world where they can shape the future of Dream Ride. Aspiring writers, talented illustrators, and creative paper engineers converge on our platform, working hand in hand to bring new chapters and captivating visuals to life.

By becoming a part of the Dream Ride social network, children open the door to a realm where their ideas and proposals hold significance. They have the opportunity to contribute to the ongoing narrative, sharing their imaginative concepts and fueling the creation of future editions. It is a place where their voices are heard, and their visions shape the destiny of Dream Ride.

Beyond the realms of creativity, Dream Ride also fosters valuable life skills. Collaboration and teamwork are at the core of our community, offering young minds the chance to learn how to cooperate and communicate effectively with their peers. Through collaborative projects, shared ideas, and mutual support, children develop essential teamwork experience that will serve them well in future endeavours.

Dream Ride is a catalyst for unleashing the full potential of children's creativity, nurturing their imaginations, and fostering a sense of collaboration that will guide them throughout their lives. Join us on this remarkable journey where dreams come alive, friendships blossom, and young minds are empowered to shape their own adventures.
